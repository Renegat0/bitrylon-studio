The Game

What is Spirelon?

Spirelon is an action-packed Vertical Platformer for Android. Climb as high as possible before the camera swallows you whole. Jump from platform to platform, dodge dangerous enemies and unleash your hero's unique abilities to reach brand-new heights.

The higher you climb, the faster the camera scrolls and the more aggressive enemies become. Pick one of six distinct heroes, conquer 50 missions and fight your way to the top of the global leaderboard.
